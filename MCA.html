I am very surprised to see how closely related to the real word scenarios, applications and problems the structure of OS is. Thanks to the OS teacher Prof. Vishnu Priya, who has been great at relating the real world situations to the functioning of the OS, I have developed a keen interest in figuring out the intricacies of functioning of Operating System.
